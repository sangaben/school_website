About Our Main Campus

Located on Nebbi Highway opposite Ceford, our Main Campus provides a complete educational pathway from Nursery through Secondary education. With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, we offer a nurturing environment for students at every stage of development.

The Main Campus is known for its vibrant learning community, excellent academic results, and wide range of co-curricular activities. Our holistic approach ensures students develop intellectually, socially, and emotionally.

Grade Levels Offered

Early Years (Nursery)

Play-based learning program focusing on social, emotional, and cognitive development for children aged 3-5 years.

Lower Primary (P1-P3)

Foundational learning with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and social skills development.

Upper Primary (P4-P7)

Advanced curriculum preparing pupils for secondary education with specialized subject teachers.
