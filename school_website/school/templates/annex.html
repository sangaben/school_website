About Our Annex Campus

Located in the peaceful Obolokofuku neighborhood, our Annex Campus is specifically designed for early childhood education, offering programs from Nursery through Primary 3. Our campus features child-sized facilities, colorful learning spaces, and experienced early childhood educators.

We focus on creating a warm, nurturing environment where children develop a love for learning through play-based and hands-on activities. Our small class sizes ensure individual attention for each child.

Grade Levels Offered

Nursery School

For children aged 3-5 years, focusing on social skills, early literacy, and creative expression through play.

Kindergarten

Transition program preparing children for primary school with foundational academic skills.

Lower Primary (P1-P3)

Foundational learning with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and social development.

Upper Primary (P4-P7)

Advanced curriculum with specialized teachers and cultural studies.
