About Our Golden Campus

Our Golden Campus in Koboko Town offers education from Nursery through Primary 7, with a special emphasis on bilingual education and cultural exchange. We serve both local and international families in the region.

The campus combines our proven educational approach with local cultural elements, creating a unique learning experience that prepares students for success in both local and global contexts.

Grade Levels Offered

Early Years (Nursery)

Bilingual early childhood program focusing on language development through play.

Lower Primary (P1-P3)

Foundational bilingual education with emphasis on both English and local languages.

Upper Primary (P4-P7)

Advanced curriculum with specialized teachers and cultural studies.
