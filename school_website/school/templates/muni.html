About Our Muni Campus

Located in the peaceful Muni neighborhood of Arua, our Muni Campus specializes in primary education for students from Primary 1 through Primary 7. We provide a child-friendly learning environment that balances academic rigor with creative exploration.

Our campus features spacious classrooms, dedicated play areas, and experienced teachers who understand the unique needs of primary school students. The Muni Campus is known for its warm, family-like atmosphere where every child feels valued and supported.

Grade Levels Offered

Lower Primary (P1-P3)

Foundational learning with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and social skills development.

Upper Primary (P4-P7)

Advanced curriculum preparing pupils for secondary education with specialized subject teachers.
