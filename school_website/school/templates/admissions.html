Our Admission Process

1

Inquiry

Submit an online inquiry form or contact our admissions office to schedule a school tour and receive detailed information.

2

Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school records if applicable, health records).

3

Assessment

Nursery: Informal play-based observation
Primary: Basic skills assessment in literacy and numeracy

4

Admission Decision

You will receive notification of the admission decision within 5 business days after assessment.

5

Enrollment

Complete enrollment paperwork and submit tuition deposit to secure your child's place.
